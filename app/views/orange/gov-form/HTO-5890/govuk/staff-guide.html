Access to Work Plus

Access to Work is testing ways of providing support for people with high in-work support needs. This is called Access to Work Plus. It includes funding for:

  • employers who are willing to make large workplace adjustments or changes to job roles
  • support Access to Work may not have paid for in the past

This will not affect eligibility for other Access to Work support.

If you are supporting someone who you think may qualify for Access to Work Plus, you can use the referral form to let us know.

Referral form

The person you are supporting needs to apply for Access to Work before you send the referral form.

You will need to give:

  • your name and contact details
  • details of your relationship to the person you are referring
  • the date they applied for Access to Work and their application reference – these will be in their application confirmation messages (optional)
  • their job title and workplace for the job listed on their Access to Work application

If you need to contact Access to Work

You can contact Access to Work for free on:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • Textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • Relay UK if you cannot hear or speak on the phone: 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • Video Relay Service for British Sign Language – find out how to use the Video Relay Service
